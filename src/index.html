About Us

Who We Are

We are Fanos Lehulu Charity Organization, a dedicated non-profit committed to serving our community with compassion, integrity, and excellence. Our organization focuses on education, environmental protection, community service quality, and special care for those who need it most. Established in 2013 E.C. as “Fanos Lehulu Charity Organization,” in accordance with Proclamation No. 1113/2011 E.C.

Our Vision

Improving lives and opportunity for vulnerable communities, with special focus on women, children, and elderly, through education, environmental protection, economic advancement, psychological healing, and comprehensive care.

Our Mission

We strengthen our community’s economic capacity by organizing savings groups, facilitating diverse income-generating projects, mobilizing resources and sponsorships, and coordinating with governmental and non-governmental charitable organizations to implement joint initiatives.

1. Help students who need support to obtain adequate educational resources and acquire better knowledge

2. Protect environmental and natural resources, prevent damage to our community, and make the agricultural sector productive by providing professional and financial support

3. Provide comprehensive support for health service quality and accessibility in the areas where the organization operates

4. Identify elderly, women, and children who need special support and ensure they receive necessary care and assistance

Our Services

Educational Support

We provide educational resources, scholarships, and learning materials to students who need support to achieve their academic goals.

Environmental Protection

Environmental conservation programs, tree planting initiatives, and community awareness campaigns to protect our natural resources.

Health Services

Comprehensive health service support, medical assistance, and healthcare accessibility programs for vulnerable communities.

Special Care Programs

Dedicated support and care services for elderly, women, and children who require special attention and assistance.
